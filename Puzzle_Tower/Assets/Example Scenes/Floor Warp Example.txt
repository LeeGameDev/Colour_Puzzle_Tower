Unity Scene Name: Floor Warp Example.unity

How to use
==========
- Walk with WASD
- Rotate camera with mouse
- Select warp floor with 1, 2, 3, and 4

1. Walk to the 1st floor warp
2. Walk up the slope to the next floor's warp
3. Repeat step 2 until you reach the top floor's warp
4. While standing on a warp press 1, 2, 3, or 4 to instantly warp to that floor's warp location.

Note:
- Cloak is disabled for this example.

- The player can only warp to locations to which they have been.

- The player must stand on the warp location in order to warp elsewhere.

- The player must stand on the warp location in order for it to be added to the list of available warp locations.
